About Flexswitch

"Flexswitch is an adaptive footwear concept that adjusts to the wearer’s foot size and shape. Our aim is to reduce sizing friction, extend product life, and improve comfort and accessibility across users."

:: after

The Concept

"The Flexswitch prototype combines a flexible sole structure with adjustable upper components to expand or contract across a defined size range. The intended benefits include fewer size‑specific purchases for families, lower return rates for retailers, and broader accessibility for contexts such as rentals and humanitarian supply chains. Our current course project focuses on a functional front‑end demonstration and a physical or virtual mock‑up to illustrate the adjustment mechanism and user experience."
